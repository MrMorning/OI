C. Trails and Glades

4 seconds
memory limit per test
256 megabytes
input
standard input
output
standard output

Vasya went for a walk in the park. The park has n glades, numbered from 1 to n. There are m trails between the glades. The trails are numbered from 1 to m, where the i-th trail connects glades xi and yi.
